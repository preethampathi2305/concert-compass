Our Services

Personalized Music

We provide personalized music recommendations to our users based on their music preferences. Our recommendation engine takes into account various factors such as music genres they enjoy, and artists they follow.

Read More

Concert Discovery

We help users discover new artists and bands performing at music festivals and concerts. We offer recommendations for events that users have already purchased tickets to, allowing them to make the most of their festival experience.

Read More

Plugin Integration

Our platform can be integrated as a plugin to music festival apps and existing music streaming platforms like Spotify and Apple Music. This enables us to leverage their large user bases and offer our services to a wider audience.

Read More

Seamless User Experience

Our platform offers a seamless user experience, making it easy for users to discover new artists and make informed decisions about which events to attend. We strive to provide a user-friendly interface that is intuitive and easy to use.

About

Our platform, ConcertCompass, recommends similar artists for music festival-goers based on their music preferences, adding value to users who have bought tickets to entire events. We aim to integrate with music festival apps and streaming platforms to reach a wider audience.


Key Features

  • Discover new artists at festivals
  • Personalized recommendations
  • Integrates with music apps
  • Analyze listening habits
  • Enhances festival experience

More info

Our team comprises experts in data science, software engineering, and backend development.
